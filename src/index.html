Who We Are

Black Cat Farms is a partner operated farm established in 2021 that aims to provide more nutritious (and more delicious) locally grown produce to North Texas families while not compromising on responsible environmental stewardship. We believe rethinking what we eat, and how it is grown, is an integral part of leading a healthy, sustainable lifestyle.

How We Do It

Our methodology prioritizes sustainability and local ecology. We do this by combining the best aspects of the following 3 schools of thought:

Biointensive Farming

The practice of designing fields to be worked by humans, not machines. This allows for much more efficient use of land and water by arranging plants close together.

Regenerative Agriculture

A holistic land management practice that leverages photosynthesis to close the carbon cycle and biological pest management to reduce the impact of human activities on the environment.

Korean Natural Farming

A self-sufficient farming system that involves culturing indigenous microorganisms (IMO) and DIY inputs in place of tilling and manufactured fertilizers. We feed the soil, not the plants.
